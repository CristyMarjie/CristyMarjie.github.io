    • The Concept

    This logo was made for a davao based online shop. Jah and Irie mainly sells baby essentials. In creating the logo, the client sent a sample photo of the feel she wants for her logo. So based on her preference, I used watercolor strokes using Adobe Illustrator.

    The client had plenty of options and requested a couple of revisions until the logo was finalized.
